About the HUB

The HUB is a convenient way to stack —like cards in a deck— my online content. As an artist/designer I have hundreds of pages, artists, and blogs that I have bookmarked for creative references. When I have a creativity block and need inspiration, I often start opening all these many bookmarks. Since most of my bookmarks are rarely opened, I often forget why I bookmarked it in the first place. The HUB is my solution to the hundreds of bookmarked pages. Now I can create a card for every item I would have normally bookmarked. Unlike bookmarks, I can also stack up images, quotes, blog posts, videos, or other content.

Red tops are art related cards

Green tops are design related cards

Blue tops are code related cards

Red bottoms are important cards

Let's get the creavity flowing with the HUB.
